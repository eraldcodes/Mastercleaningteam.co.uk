End of Tenancy Cleaning

 

Master Cleaning Team offer a full end of tenancy cleaning service for landlords and tenants. A professionally cleaned property will be more attractive for fast re-letting. And if you’re renting a house, it ensures you receive 100% of your deposit back.

We are experienced post tenancy cleaners, providing an exceptional service at affordable rates.

Deep cleans

 

Move-out cleaning requires a lot of elbow-grease and attention to detail. Our cleaners are highly-trained professionals, who’ll get the job done diligently and efficiently in a safe environment.

With many years’ experience, our after tenancy team will leave you with a pristine clean property that’s ready for re-letting, or inspection. We’ll clean from top to bottom, getting rid of dust, dirt, grime, bacteria and stains including:
  • Doors
  • Windows
  • Flooring
  • Skirtings
  • Radiators
  • Light fittings
  • Switches/sockets
  • Appliances
  

…and carpets and upholstery

  Our comprehensive service is designed to meet all your cleaning needs. We can deep cleanse your carpets and steam clean sofas and curtains, removing dirt and stains for an additional fee. That means there’s no hassle or extra expense in employing separate service providers.

Competitive prices

  End of tenancy cleaners cost £14 per hour, for a minimum of 4 hours per visit. These rates are great value for money; we constantly review our pricing to ensure we’re one of the most cost-effective agencies in London.
